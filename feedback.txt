Great work Nithin.  I would advise a couple things for the future.  Mostly just about coding style.
Make sure to check all your outputs (Even when it has 2 numbers is missing it's brackets).
Strings already have methods to help you find characters so you don't have to search with a for loop.  Could have made your life a bit easier.
You can also take advantage of existing functions to do things for you (ceil and floor for instance so you don't need if statements)
Since the brackets never go anywhere in the menu you can also just make your menu variable hold the "number" or "number to number"
